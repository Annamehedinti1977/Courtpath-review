What CourtPath is

A practical plain-language legal workflow product.

CourtPath is designed to help people move through legal and court-related processes with more clarity, structure, and confidence.

Instead of forcing users to decode scattered instructions, it organizes the journey into guided intake, matter tracking, document readiness, next actions, and escalation points when human help is needed.

The project has already moved beyond idea stage into product definition, prototype design, and technical packaging for a future implementation.
