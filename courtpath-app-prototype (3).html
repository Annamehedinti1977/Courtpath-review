A court workflow app built for real people under real pressure.

CourtPath turns a stressful situation into an organized matter, showing what happened, what is missing, what is urgent, and when human help is needed. The prototype is designed around plain-language intake, filing readiness, accessible mobile workflows, and self-help support principles.

Why this intake feels different

Plain-language first
The workflow starts with the person’s situation, not a court form number.

Return later logic
Multi-session processes should support pausing and resuming without losing the user’s place.
